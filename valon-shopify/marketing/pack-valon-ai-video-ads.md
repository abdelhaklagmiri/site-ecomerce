## Assembly workflow (all ads)

### Step 1 — Generate clips
| Tool | Best for | Tip |
|------|----------|-----|
| **Veo 3** | Photorealistic UGC, Moroccan faces | Generate 8s clips, extend or chain in editor |
| **Kling** | Product motion from your unboxing photo | Upload packaging photo as first frame |
| **Runway** | Cinematic transitions, before/after | Use Motion Brush on hair for shine transition |

### Step 2 — Edit in CapCut / Premiere
1. Import 3–4 clips per ad
2. Add FR subtitles (Darija VO recorded separately or AI voice)
3. Overlay: `599 DH` + `COD` + `valon-shop.com`
4. Music: royalty-free lo-fi or trending Meta audio (low volume)
5. Export **1080×1920** H.264

### Step 3 — VO recording (Darija)
Record on phone or use ElevenLabs with Moroccan Arabic accent. Match timing table per ad.

### Step 4 — Meta upload
- **Placement:** Instagram Reels, Facebook Reels, Stories
- **Objective:** Sales or ThruPlay
- **CTA button:** Shop Now → pack-valon product page
- **Primary text:** `غدي تلاحظ تحول رهيب في شعرك · 599 DH · الدفع عند الاستلام · Livraison gratuite`
